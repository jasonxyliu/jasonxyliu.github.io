I am a computer science Ph.D. student at Brown University. I am fortunately to be advised by Prof. Stefanie Tellex and to work with Prof. George Konidaris.

Research Interests

I am interested in building intelligent agents that can perform lone-horizon tasks in various environments provided with sparse reward signals. To that end, I would like to leverage structures in natural language and perception to help robots plan and learn correctly and efficiently.
